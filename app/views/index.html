This prototype details itterations of the application process users will go through to apply for compensation if they have suffered an injury through being a victim of violent crime.

Defining our Minimum Viable Product Sprint 14 & 15

Simple journey

The focus of this journey is to see how simple we can make the application process by getting all but the simplest users out of the journey at the start.

Users that do not fall in to the simple group will be nudged in other directions. We have yet to define those other directions.

This journey focuses on users who:

  • are direct applicants
  • are over 18
  • are British citizens
  • do not have any criminal convictions
  • have applied for compensation from other sources
  • do not have ongoing medical treatment
  • have suffered personal injury
