Applying for special expenses

In applying for special expenses, you confirm the child experienced one of the following for a period of six months or more because of the crime. They:

  • lost money from their job as they could not attend work
  • were unable to work due to their injuries

The period of six months or more must cover 197 days or more (28 full weeks). This can be a single period of time or cover several different periods of time.

If the child was not of working age (16 or older), you can still apply for special expenses as the crime can impact their future working capacity.

Special expenses they can apply for include:

  • physical aids lost or damaged during the incident
  • cost of treatments delivered by the NHS
  • cost of treatments delivered by a state health service outside the United Kingdom
  • special equipment
  • required changes to the home as a result of the incident
  • cost of care that resulted from the incident
  • cost of supervision by care professionals
  • cost of administrators who handle their affairs
