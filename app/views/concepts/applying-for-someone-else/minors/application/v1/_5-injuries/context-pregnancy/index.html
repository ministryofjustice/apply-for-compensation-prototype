Pregnancy

We need to ask a few questions about pregnancy related to the crime. This helps us better understand the case and if we need to ask for more evidence from you. It also ensures you’re considered for all correct compensation from us.

These questions may be difficult to answer or may not be relevant to this application. You can choose not to share this information with us by answering the following questions "No". This means we may not be able to pay compensation to you for this part of the application.

Your privacy and confidentiality

We may need to contact you or the police if we need to get more information about this. We will not share this information with anyone else.
