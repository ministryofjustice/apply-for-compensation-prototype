Our responsibility under the Equality Act 2010

The Equality Act requires us to make sure our policies and services advance equality of opportunity, foster good relations between different groups, and eliminate unlawful discrimination, harrassment and victimisation.

There are nine protected characteristics under the Equality Act

  • age
  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • marriage and civil partnership
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex
  • sexual orientation
