Who has authority to apply for the victim?

You need to prove you have parental responsibility or legal authority for the victim to apply.

To prove this, you'll need relevant documents your name is on, such as:

  • the victim’s full birth certificate adoption documents
  • a parental responsibility agreement
  • a court order showing you have parental responsibility, which means you have legal authority, for the victim

We need proof so we know you have the right to apply on the victim’s behalf. We'll tell you how to send this at the end of the application.

If you’re still unsure about parental responsibility and what it means, you can find out more on the UK Government website (opens in new tab).
