Why we ask you to provide the victim’s telephone number

We need the victim’s telephone number in the event we cannot reach you via the contact preferences you have provided.

We will never contact the victim in the first instance. This will only ever be after we’ve tried all of your contact preferences exhaustively.

We may also use it to contact the victim if they no longer want you to represent them.
