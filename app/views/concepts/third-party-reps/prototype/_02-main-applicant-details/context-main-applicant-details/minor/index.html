The person with parental responsibility for the victim

We’re going to ask for details about the person who has parental responsibility for the child in this application. This means they have the legal authority to apply on their behalf.

This may be:

  • The child’s birth, step or adoptive parent
  • A person named on a Special Guardianship order
  • Social worker
  • Local authority

We ask for the personal and contact details of this person.
