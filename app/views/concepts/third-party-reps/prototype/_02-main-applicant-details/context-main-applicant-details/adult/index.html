The person with legal authority for the victim

We’re going to ask for details about the person who has legal authority for the victim in this application. This means they can apply on their behalf.

This may be:

  • power of attorney document you’re named on
  • a court order showing you have legal authority for the victim
  • Social worker
  • Local authority

We ask for the personal and contact details of this person.
