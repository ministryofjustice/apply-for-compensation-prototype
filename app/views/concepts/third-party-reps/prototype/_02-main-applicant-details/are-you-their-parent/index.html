Can I apply if I am a foster carer?

If you are a foster carer, you can still apply to CICA for the victim. But only as their representative. Not their parent. After you’ve sent the application, we will only speak to the person or organisation who has parental responsibility for the victim. This is normally the local authority.

If you’re still unsure about parental responsibility and what it means, you can find out more on the UK Government website (opens in new tab).
