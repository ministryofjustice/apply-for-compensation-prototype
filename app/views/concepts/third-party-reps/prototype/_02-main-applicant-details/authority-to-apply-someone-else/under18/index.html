We need proof so we know they have the right to apply on the child’s behalf.

This proof can be:

  • the child’s full birth certificate
  • adoption documents
  • a parental responsibility agreement
  • a court order showing they have parental responsibility, which means they have legal authority, for the child

You can:

  • take or scan a photo and send it via email
  • send a photocopy of this to us via post

If their name or the child’s name has changed, you’ll have to show proof of this change.

We'll tell you how to send this at the end of the application.
