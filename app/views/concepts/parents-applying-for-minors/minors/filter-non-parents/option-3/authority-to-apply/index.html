You need to send proof that you’re the child’s parent

We need proof so we know you have the right to apply on the child's behalf.

This proof can be:

  • the child's full birth or adoption certificate
  • a relevant document showing you're the child's parent

You can:

  • take or scan a photo and send it via email
  • send a photocopy of this to us via post

If your name or the child’s name has changed, you’ll have to show proof of this change.

We'll tell you how you to send this at the end of the application.
