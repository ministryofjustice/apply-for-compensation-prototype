You need to send proof that you’re the victim’s parent

We need proof so we know you have the right to apply on the victim's behalf.

This proof can be:

  • the victim's full birth or adoption certificate
  • a relevant document showing you're the victim's parent

You can:

  • take or scan a photo and send it via email
  • send a photocopy of this to us via post

If your name or the victim’s name has changed, you’ll have to show proof of this change.

We'll tell you how you to send this at the end of the application.
