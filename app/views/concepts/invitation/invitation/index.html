New way to claim compensation

There’s a new, simpler way to claim criminal injuries compensation.

We need your help to get people using the new application form.

Benefits

The new application form is:

  • less traumatic - the applicant does not have to give details about what happened to them
  • shorter - around half as many questions
  • easier - the questions are easier to read

Who it's for

It's for people who are:

  • victims of sexual assault or abuse
  • over 18
  • applying without a named representative

Find out more

Call Julie Allan on 07889 401 510 or email julie.allan@cica.gov.uk to find out more.

The new service is in a ‘private beta’ phase. This means you can only use it by invitation, so we can check it’s working well with a small number of users.
