Make a 'same roof' rule enquiry

This enquiry form is for victims of violent crime who lived with the person who attacked them between 1 August 1964 and 1 October 1979. It includes those who, because of the ‘same roof’ rule:

  • had compensation claims rejected
  • did not apply for compensation
! Warning If the ‘same roof’ rule did not affect you, you must use our online application service.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your previous application reference numbers if you have applied before
  • a phone number we contact you on

You should be aware that we will:

  • only contact you by telephone
  • assess your application against the requirements of the current Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme
Start

Other ways to make a 'same roof' rule enquiry

If you cannot use the online form there are other ways you can start your enquiry.

You will have to provide your:

  • previous application reference numbers, if you know them
  • full name
  • previous names, if any
  • date of birth
  • current address
  • telephone number
  • preferred time for us to call you back, for example, Monday between 12pm and 5pm
