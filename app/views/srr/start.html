Request a 'same roof' rule call-back

Use this form if you were a victim of violent crime and:

  • the crime happened between 1 August 1964 and 30 September 1979
  • you lived as a family member under the same roof as the assailant at that time

This applies to those who, because of the ‘same roof’ rule:

  • had compensation claims rejected
  • did not apply for compensation

Use our online application service if:

  • the ‘same roof’ rule did not affect you
  • you do not want us to call you
  • you are a legal representative

Before you start

You will need a phone number we can call you back on

You should be aware that:

  • initial contact will be by telephone
  • phone calls will come from a number with no caller ID
  • applications will be assessed under the 2012 Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme
