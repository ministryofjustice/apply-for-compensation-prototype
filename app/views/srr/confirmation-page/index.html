Thank you for submitting your request.

What happens next

We will call you back on 07595922929 within 10 days.

The call should take around 40 minutes to complete and we'll aim to call you at your preferred days and times.

Prepare for our call

When we call back we may ask you for more information about the crime. This may include:

  • the date and location of the crime
  • the name of the police station where the crime was reported
  • your crime reference number
  • your GP’s name and address
  • your dentist’s name and address (if you had dental treatment because of your injuries)
  • details of any previous applications you have made to CICA
  • details of your injuries
  • details of any unspent criminal convictions
  • details of the person who abused you
  • proof of your identity
