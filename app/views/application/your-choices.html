Option 1

If you choose option 1 we will get the police report and may also need:

  • medical reports from your doctor about any physical injuries
  • a report from a clinical psychologist about any mental injuries
  • evidence of any loss of earnings or other expenses

Option 2

If you choose option 2 we will:

  • only need to get the police report
  • use the information in the report to make a decision
  • give you a decision within 16 weeks

Option 2 will not consider any other injuries, losses or expenses you may have.
