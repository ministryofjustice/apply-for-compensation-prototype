Any compensation we pay for sexual assault and abuse already contains an element that recognises your mental trauma and distress. If you want to apply for compensation for additional mental trauma we will need more evidence.

We can pay compensation for additional mental trauma that had a significant negative effect on your day to day life such as:

  • performance at work or school
  • personal and social relationships
  • sexual relationships
