Victims of sexual assault: your options

We pay compensation for crimes of sexual violence in recognition of the distress and mental trauma suffered. We make payments under a tariff system dependant on the nature of the assault.

If you choose this option we'll:

  • assess your eligibility
  • ask the police to send us the crime report
  • not need your medical records

Do not choose this option if you want to claim for:

  • physical injuries
  • disabling mental injuries
  • loss of earnings or special expenses

These applications are more complex and take longer to decide as we usually need additional information to make a decision.
