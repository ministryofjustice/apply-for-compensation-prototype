Declaration

CICA must process applications applying the rules of the Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme. I understand this requires CICA to share and obtain information about me from:

  • the police, prosecuting authorities, fraud prevention agencies and ACRO Criminal Records Office (including to carry out convictions checks on me and, in fatal injury applications, the deceased)
  • medical authorities and practitioners (including police doctors and surgeons)
  • the representative (if any) named by me
  • any other third party where necessary to process my application (including to obtain an expert medical or other expert report)

You can find out more about how we handle your information in our privacy notice

  • I confirm that the information I will give CICA is true and complete
  • I accept that online communications constitute ‘in writing’ for the purposes of the Scheme
  • I agree to notify CICA of any change in my circumstances
  • I understand that if I knowingly give information that is misleading or incorrect, CICA may withhold or reduce any award and I may be liable to prosecution or other action
