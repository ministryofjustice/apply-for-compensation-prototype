How we assess your application

We can access your application by confirming the details of the assault with  {% if data ['police-force'] %} {{ data['police-force'] }} {% else %} Humberside {% endif %} Police

Assessing your application like this means we:

  • do not have to ask you to get medical reports from your doctor
  • do not have to ask you to undergo a psychological assessment
  • in most cases can give you a decision in 16 weeks

If you are eligible for compensation, we'll offer you a payment based on Part B of The Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme. This takes into account the mental trauma you've suffered.

We will not take anything else into account when assessing your application.
