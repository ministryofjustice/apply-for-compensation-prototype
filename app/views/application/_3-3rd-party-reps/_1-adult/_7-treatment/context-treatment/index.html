Their treatment

We’re going to ask for some details about their treatment.

We’ll use these to understand:

  • if they've told their GP about their injuries
  • who has evidence of their injuries

We often have to ask their GP or other health service provider for evidence about their injuries and treatment. We will let you know if we need to do this.
