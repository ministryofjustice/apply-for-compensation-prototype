Help understanding care and supervision

Care

This means they cannot do daily tasks themselves. For example, care may include but is not limited to.

  • preparing meals
  • help to eat
  • help going to the bathroom
  • taking medication
  • administering medical treatments

Supervision

This means they pose a substantial danger to themselves or others without supervision.
