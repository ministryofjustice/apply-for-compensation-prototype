The person responsible for the applicant

We’re going to ask for details about the person who is responsible for the applicant you’re applying on behalf of.

Applying for an adult

If you’re applying for an adult, the person responsible for them may be their:

  • parent
  • partner
  • friend
  • other relative

Applying for a child

If you’re applying for a child, the person responsible for them may be their:

  • birth, step or adoptive parent
  • social worker
  • foster carer
  • local authority representative

The information we ask for includes their personal and contact details.
