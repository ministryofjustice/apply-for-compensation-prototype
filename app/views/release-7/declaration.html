Declaration

CICA must process applications applying the rules of the Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme. I understand this requires you to share and obtain information about me from:

  • the police, prosecuting authorities, fraud prevention agencies and ACRO Criminal Records Office (including to carry out convictions checks on me)
  • medical authorities and practitioners (including police doctors and surgeons)
  • my representative (if any)
  • any other third party where necessary to process my application (including expert reports)

You can find out more about how we handle your information in our privacy notice

  • I confirm that the information I will give is true and complete
  • I accept that online communications constitute ‘in writing’ for the purposes of the Scheme
  • I agree to notify you of any change in my circumstances
  • I understand that if I knowingly give information that is misleading or incorrect, you may withhold or reduce any award and I may be liable to prosecution or other action
